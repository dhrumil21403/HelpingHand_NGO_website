Using HelpingHand a user can donate the foods, clothes, money, medicines etc. without going anywhere at any time from any place. Our mission is to provide essential support and resources to individuals and families facing adversity. Through a variety of programs and initiatives, we aim to address the root causes of poverty, hunger, and inequality, and empower individuals to build brighter futures for themselves and their communities.
Our vision is to create a world where every individual, regardless of their circumstances, has the opportunity to live a dignified and fulfilling life. We envision a future where poverty, hunger, and inequality are eradicated, and where all members of society have access to the resources and support they need to thrive.
